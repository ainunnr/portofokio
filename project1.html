SIALFI

Sales management information system website (2022)

Description

Sales management information system website for managing several online and offline shops. This system gives access rights to each users depend on their roles and which shops they are responsible for. This system enables users to manage stocks, sales and reports based on their roles.

General Features

  1. Sign-up and Login.
  2. CRUD Website.
  3. Documents Download and Upload.

Tech to develop this website: Codeigniter, PHP, JQuery & AJAX, MySQL, Bootstrap.
